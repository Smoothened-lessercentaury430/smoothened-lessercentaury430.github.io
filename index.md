## 🔒 Understanding local processing

Most web tools upload your files to a cloud server. This is a risk. Your data travels over the internet. A company holds your information on their drive. 

compress-pro does not work this way. This software uses WebAssembly to run tools directly in your browser. Think of this like running a small office inside your computer. The app receives the file from your folder. It works on the file. It saves the new version back to your folder. The file never travels to our servers. No one sees your business documents or personal photos. Your privacy remains secure at all times.

## ❓ Frequently asked questions

Do I need a fast internet connection?
No. Once you download the software, you do not need the internet to make your files smaller. Everything happens on your local hardware.

Will I lose quality?
The software provides settings to manage quality. You can choose a balance between file size and image clarity. Most users choose the default setting which gives good results for standard use.

Can I compress multiple files at once?
Yes. Drag and drop a group of files into the window to process them as a batch. This saves time when you organize photo libraries or document collections.

Does it work with large videos?
Yes. The software handles high-definition video files. Large files take more time to process than small photos. Keep the app open while it works.

Is my data safe during a crash?
Yes. Because the app does not send files away, a crash only affects the active task. Your original files remain in your folder untouched. The application only saves a new copy when it finishes the task. It does not overwrite your original files unless you choose to replace them.
